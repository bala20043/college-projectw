About Our Institution

Leading technical education with excellence and innovation

Our Legacy

Tamil Nadu Government Polytechnic Diploma College, Madurai, established in 1957, stands as a beacon of technical education in South India. With over six decades of excellence, we have been nurturing skilled professionals who contribute significantly to the industrial and technological advancement of our nation.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development of students to meet global challenges.

Mission
  • Provide quality technical education with industry-relevant curriculum
  • Foster research and innovation among students and faculty
  • Develop ethical and socially responsible engineers
  • Establish strong industry-academia partnerships
Accreditation & Recognition
  • Autonomous status granted by AICTE
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Institution
  • NAAC Accredited with 'A' Grade
  • Affiliated to Tamil Nadu State Board of Technical Education
